Two URL inputs are decoded as GTFS-RT FeedMessage + VehiclePosition in your browser using protobuf.js (inlined) + the GTFS-RT v2.0 schema. Vehicles are matched by vehicle.id (falling back to entity.id). Numeric lat/lon are compared at 5 decimal places (about 1 metre); bearing / speed compared as integers.
